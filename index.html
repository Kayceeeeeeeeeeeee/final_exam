About me

My journey with Information Technology began with a simple curiosity. I remember as a child, being mesmerized by the way computers could bring stories to life through video games. Each pixel, each line of code, felt like a tiny magic trick, and I desperately wanted to understand the secrets behind it all. It was like learning a new language that could open up whole new worlds, and I couldn't resist the urge to explore. So, with wide-eyed wonder and a heart full of excitement, I embarked on the path of Information Technology, eager to unlock the mysteries of the digital realm.

My life as an IT student has been a journey of quiet determination. While others seemed to effortlessly grasp new concepts, I often felt like I was wading through mud, struggling to keep my head above water. The frustration of being a "slow learner" was a constant companion, gnawing at my confidence and self-esteem. Thanks to my friends, they help me a lot to these situations. They help me survive everday, and im thankful for everything they do for me.
